Features

Everything to Keep
Your Child Protected

20 features working together in one device smaller than a watch.

SOS Button

One press sends instant alert to parents with GPS location. Works even without looking at the screen.

Live GPS Tracking

Real-time location updates every 60 seconds. See your child on a live map in the parent dashboard.

Health Monitor

Track heart rate and detect falls automatically. Sends alert if abnormal activity detected.

Geofencing

Set safe zones. Get alerted instantly if your child leaves the designated area.

Medical Profile

Blood type, allergies, medications, emergency contacts. Accessible via QR code or short URL.

Battery Monitor

Real-time battery level on dashboard. Low battery alerts sent to parents before it dies.

Emergency Contacts

Up to 3 emergency contacts with tap-to-call. First responders see contacts instantly.

Encrypted Data

All data transmitted over HTTPS. Token-based authentication. Your child's data stays private.

Auto-Reconnect

Loses WiFi? Automatically reconnects when available. Stores alerts offline and syncs later.

Parent Dashboard

Web-based dashboard. Live map, alert timeline, battery status, device health. No app install needed.

Fall Detection

Accelerometer detects sudden falls. Automatically sends alert with location if child doesn't respond.

SMS Alerts

Optional GSM module sends SMS directly to parents. Works without internet connection.

OLED Display

Shows battery, WiFi status, GPS fix, and last alert time. Always visible on the child's wrist.

QR Code Profile

Scan QR on the device to see full medical profile. No app needed. Works on any phone browser.

Alert History

Full timeline of every alert, heartbeat, and location update. Filterable and searchable.

Real-Time Updates

WebSocket-powered live dashboard. See alerts the instant they happen. No refresh needed.

Share Location

Share your child's live location link with trusted family members or authorities in emergencies.

Safe Zones

Define home, school, park as safe zones. Dashboard shows green when inside, alerts when outside.

Social Links

Add Instagram, GitHub, or any social link to your profile. Custom buttons with any URL.

Open Source

Fully open source. Build it yourself for under $17. Modify the firmware, add features, make it yours.
